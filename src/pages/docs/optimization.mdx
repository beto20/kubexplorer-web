Tider analyzes your workloads and surfaces heuristic recommendations to
help you right-size resources — cutting waste without sacrificing reliability.

## How it works

Tider compares requested and observed resource usage across your
workloads. Using heuristics, it flags:

- **Over-provisioned** resources requesting far more CPU or memory than they use
- **Under-provisioned** resources at risk of throttling or eviction
- **Missing requests/limits** that can lead to unpredictable scheduling

## Acting on recommendations

Each recommendation explains the reasoning and suggests a target value. Review
the suggestion, then apply it directly or copy the adjusted manifest into your
own workflow.

<aside>
Recommendations are heuristic and meant as guidance — always validate against
your workload's real requirements before applying in production.
</aside>
