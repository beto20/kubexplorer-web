When something breaks, Tider helps you find the cause quickly with
guided, heuristic diagnostics — so you spend less time debugging and more time
building.

## Guided diagnostics

The assistant inspects unhealthy resources and walks you through the most
likely causes. For a failing pod it checks common culprits such as:

- Image pull errors and missing secrets
- Crash loops and non-zero exit codes
- Failing readiness or liveness probes
- Insufficient resources or scheduling constraints

## Root-cause hints

For each issue, Tider presents a plain-language explanation, the relevant
events and logs, and suggested next steps — all in one place, without
switching back to the terminal.

## Logs in context

Stream container logs directly alongside the resource you're investigating, and
jump between related pods and events to follow the trail.
