Kubexplorer gives you live visibility into what's happening across your
clusters through intuitive dashboards and metrics.

## The dashboard

The cluster dashboard summarizes the essentials:

- **Nodes** — count, status, and capacity
- **Workloads** — deployments, statefulsets, daemonsets, and pods
- **Resource usage** — CPU and memory consumption over time

## Drilling into resources

Select any workload to see its pods, containers, live logs, and recent events.
Status indicators highlight pods that are pending, crash-looping, or
unhealthy so problems surface quickly.

## Events &amp; alerts

Kubexplorer surfaces Kubernetes events alongside the resources they relate to,
giving you context without digging through `kubectl describe`. Watch for
warning-level events to catch issues early.
